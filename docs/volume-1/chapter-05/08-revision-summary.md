## Chapter Summary

Strings, Numbers, and Dates gives you a professional foundation for text, numeric data, BigInt, regular expressions, timestamps, and time calculations. The goal is not to memorize isolated facts. The goal is to build a mental model that survives production incidents and interview pressure.

You learned the main rules, how engines and hosts participate, how memory and flow diagrams clarify behavior, how to write production-style examples, and how to discuss trade-offs with interviewer-grade precision.
