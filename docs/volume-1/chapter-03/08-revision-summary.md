## Chapter Summary

Operators and Expressions gives you a professional foundation for how JavaScript combines values, evaluates expressions, and decides precedence. The goal is not to memorize isolated facts. The goal is to build a mental model that survives production incidents and interview pressure.

You learned the main rules, how engines and hosts participate, how memory and flow diagrams clarify behavior, how to write production-style examples, and how to discuss trade-offs with interviewer-grade precision.
